Nigeria’s Adesuwa Osayaren Crowned 2018 Miss Nations of the World

  • Share on Facebook
  • Tweet on Twitter
  • +

A Nigerian has emerged winner of the 2018 Miss Nations of the World pageantry. Adesuwa Osayaren beat 11 other contestants to snatch the coveted crown on Saturday Night.

Interestingly, the beauty queen who represented Nigeria in the cultural scholarship pageantry competition, was winner of another pageantry, Miss Africa Arizona only 9 months ago.

Speaking on the Miss Nations of the World competition, co-founder Gloria Mensah told ABC 4 Utah:

The Miss Nations of the World is a pageant that not only provides a platform for international women to succeed but it also creates an opportunity for people to be tolerant of each other’s diversity.

CONGRATULATIONS to all the lovely ladies that participated in Miss Nations of the World 2018. You all did a great job. Congratulations to the following for winning the awards Miss Popular : Miss India Nations of the World Peoples Choice award: Nigeria Nations of the World Miss Congeniality: Miss Zambia Nations of the world Most Inspirational platform: Miss Rwanda Nations of the World. Best Evening gown: Miss Iran Nations of the World Best Talent : Miss Sudan Nations of the World. Community Advocate Award: Miss Mexico Nations of the World Best in Parade of Nations Outfit: Miss Boliva Nations of the World Best in Traditional outfit: Miss Vietnam Nations of the World Miss Photogenic: Miss Poland Nations of the World Best in Interview : Miss Nigeria Nations of the World Directors Award : Miss Tajikistan Nations of the World Best Ambassador to her Country: Miss USA Nations of the World. CONGRATULATIONS to our Top 5. 4th runner up: Miss Vietnam Nations of the World 3rd Runner up: Miss USA Nations of the World 2nd Runner up: Miss Tajikistan Nations of the World 1st Runner up:Miss Rwanda Nations of the World Miss Nations of the World 2018 : Miss Nigeria Nations of the World

A post shared by Miss Nations of the World (@missnationsoftheworld) on Aug 12, 2018 at 5:24pm PDT
